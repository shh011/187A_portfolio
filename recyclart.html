This is a site where users can be inspired by and share art made of recycled materials. It does not give tutorials on how to make a product because they only want to inspire but they do provide many pictures and links where you can find more information about the product. Their goal is to be a kind of portfolio based on ideas where users can brainstorm sustainability creativity. The site is awesome because these very cool ideas are coming from real people! It is amazing how items you think have no use can be tweaked and combined to make something much more valuable. Check them out at Recyclart.org!
