This is a site where users can purchase or find directions on how to build their own windowfarm. A windowfarm is an indoor, vertical, hydroponic growing system that allows for year-round in almost any window. The plants grow from the natural window light, climate control of living space, and organic liquid soil. Windowfarms is a blog where readers can find a lot of useful information of sustainable living and reducing carbon footprint in general. Check them out at Windowfarms.org!
